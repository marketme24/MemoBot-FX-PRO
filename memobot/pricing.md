# AuraBot - Pricing & Benefits Guide

Welcome to AuraBot! We offer flexible trading tiers designed for every type of trader, from beginners to institutions. Our pricing model aligns our success with yours.

## Subscription Tiers & Performance Fees

### 1. Starter (Free)
- **Subscription:** $0 / month
- **Performance Fee:** 0% on profitable trades
- **Features:** 
  - Paper Trading Access Only
  - $100,000 Demo Balance
  - Manual Trading (No Automated Bot)
  - Basic Market Analytics
- *Ideal for:* Beginners learning the ropes who want to test the simulator with a $100K virtual balance.

### 2. Professional ($49/month)
- **Subscription:** $49 / month
- **Performance Fee:** 2% on profitable trades
- **Features:** 
  - 5 Bot Instances
  - AI Sentiment Auto-Filter
  - Advanced SMC Indicators
  - 24/7 Priority Execution
- *Ideal for:* Dedicated traders who need robust automated tools and lower performance fees.

### 3. Institutional ($199/month)
- **Subscription:** $199 / month
- **Performance Fee:** 0% on profitable trades (Keep 100% of profits)
- **Features:** 
  - Unlimited Bot Instances
  - Enterprise API Access

- *Ideal for:* Prop firms, funds, and high-volume traders who want maximum flexibility and zero backend fees.

### 4. Lifetime Mini ($1000 One-Time)
- **Subscription:** $1000 (Lifetime Access)
- **Performance Fee:** 1% on profitable trades
- **Features:** 
  - 1 Bot Instance (Rate Limited)

  - One-Time Payment, Lifetime Access
- *Ideal for:* Users who want premium enterprise features in a single, dedicated bot without monthly commitments.

## How Performance Fees Work (The "Win-Win" Model)
Performance fees are exclusively calculated on **profitable** trades. If a trade does not result in a profit, **zero fees** are applied. This ensures our incentives are directly aligned with your success. 

As your bot closes winning trades in the Live Market, fees are automatically calculated based on your tier and added to your **Invoices** tab inside the application. You can pay these securely to keep your node operating flawlessly.

## Upgrading & Downgrading
You can change your subscription tier at any time from the **Billing** page. If you choose the Lifetime Mini plan, your account is permanently unlocked for premium features on your dedicated node.
